About Me

Kevin is a Hmong American multidisciplinary artist from the Twin Cities, where he works primarily in the mediums of spoken word, filmmaking, and theater.Kevin is a member of Street Stops and Mountain Tops, an organization of Hmong artists and organizers sharing Hip Hop and music based curriculum with Hmong communities in Southeast Asia. Kevin finds great inspiration unraveling his experience as a Hmong soul born in the United States and has a great fondness for almond milk.
